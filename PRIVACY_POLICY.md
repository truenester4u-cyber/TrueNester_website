## 13. California Resident Rights (CCPA)

### 13.1 Specific Rights for Californians

**Right to Know** (1798.100):
- What personal information we collect
- Categories of sources
- Purpose of collection
- Categories of third parties

**Request Timeline**: 45 days (extendable 45 days)

**Right to Delete** (1798.105):
- Request deletion of personal information
- Exceptions: Contract fulfillment, legal obligations
- Child data: Automatic deletion

**Request Timeline**: 45 days

**Right to Opt-Out** (1798.120):
- Opt-out of data "sale"
- Opt-out of "profiling"
- Applies to 3rd-party sharing
- Format: "Do Not Sell My Information" link

**Right to Non-Discrimination** (1798.125):
- Cannot penalize for exercising rights
- Cannot deny service for privacy requests
- Cannot charge different prices
- Cannot retaliate for complaints
